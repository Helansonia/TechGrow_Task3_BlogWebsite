Ethical Hacking – The Good Side of Cybersecurity

Published on: March 12, 2025

While hacking is often associated with cybercrime, ethical hackers use their skills to improve security.

How Ethical Hackers Help:

  • Perform penetration testing to detect security flaws.
  • Assist companies in securing their networks.
  • Follow legal and ethical hacking guidelines.

Famous Ethical Hackers:

  • Kevin Mitnick: Former hacker turned cybersecurity expert.
  • Charlie Miller: Discovered vulnerabilities in Apple products.
  • Saket Modi: Indian entrepreneur specializing in cybersecurity training.

Ethical hacking is a crucial field in today’s digital age, ensuring better security for all.
